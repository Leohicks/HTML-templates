PCIE Cable 70cm

6 pin to 8 pin (6+2)

Compatible with all high-end video cards. Because the additional 2 pins are ground, this cable can be used as a 6 pin to 6 pin cable. Ideal for GPU and ASIC mining.

  • Gauge：18AWG
  • Wire Type: Stranded Copper
  • Construction: THHN (Thermoplastic High Heat-resistant Nylon-coated)
  • Coating: Rated for 80°C/176°F
  • Connector A: 6 Pin PCIe Male
  • Connector B: 8 Pin PCIe Male (6+2)
  • Yellow (positive) and Black (ground)
